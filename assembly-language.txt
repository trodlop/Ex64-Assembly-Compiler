LDI | load immediate    | loads a value into a register               |
----|-------------------|---------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------
CLR | clear             | clear a register (makes it 0)               |
----|-------------------|---------------------------------------------|
ADD | add               | sums 2 values and saves result              |
----|-------------------|---------------------------------------------|
SUB | subtract          | subtracts 2 values and saves the result     |
----|-------------------|---------------------------------------------|
MUL | multiply          | multiplies 2 values and saves the result    |
----|-------------------|---------------------------------------------|
DIV | divide            | divides 2 values and saves the result       |
----|-------------------|---------------------------------------------|
INC | increment         | adds 1 to a value                           |
----|-------------------|---------------------------------------------|
DEC | decrement         | subtracts 1 from a value                    |
